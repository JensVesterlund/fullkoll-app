# Full Koll – receipts, gift cards, budgets, and subscriptions under control

## Problem
Receipts get lost, gift cards expire, subscriptions keep charging in the background, and budgets slip when life gets busy. You want control—without spending hours in spreadsheets or complicated tools.

## Solution
Full Koll brings everything together. Scan paper receipts with OCR, store digital receipts, track gift card balances and expirations, follow your budget, and get reminders before direct debit/subscriptions are charged. Share expenses with family or friends—fast, transparent, and fair.

## Features by module
- Receipts
  - OCR scanning from camera or photos (PDF/JPG)
  - Search and filter by merchant, date, amount, and category
  - Export as CSV or PDF (entire history or selected receipts)

- Gift Cards
  - Store number, balance, and expiration with clear reminders
  - Sensitive data is masked; temporary reveal with re‑auth for 60s

- Budget
  - Monthly budget with clear overview and trends
  - Interactive charts with caching/memoization for snappy views
  - Export any month to PDF

- Direct Debit & Subscriptions
  - Track recurring payments with due dates
  - Smart alerts before charges so you avoid surprises

- Split Bills
  - Share receipts or expenses with others
  - Clear rules and history for who paid what

- Scanning and QR/barcodes
  - OCR for text and receipt data, support for QR/barcodes

## Privacy & control
- Local reminders; you decide notifications
- Export your data (CSV/PDF) anytime
- “Do Not Track” is respected for analytics
- Privacy policy available in‑app and at /legal/privacy

## Contact & support
Need help? Open the in‑app privacy/help page at /legal/privacy for contact details and support links.

Full Koll is designed to be fast, clear, and reliable—so you spend less time searching for receipts and more time on what matters.
